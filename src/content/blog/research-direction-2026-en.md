I want my research direction to stay grounded in systems that can be measured, tuned, and explained clearly.

That means I care about more than a strong simulation result. I care about whether the hardware can be brought up safely, whether a control strategy survives real measurement noise, and whether another person can reproduce the experiment from the documentation.

The more advanced a converter platform becomes, the more important those habits become. Good research is not only about originality. It is also about clarity, repeatability, and engineering judgment.
