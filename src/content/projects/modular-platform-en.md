## Why this project matters

This project reflects the kind of research infrastructure I want to build: not a one-off prototype, but a platform that supports comparison, iteration, and documentation.

## What I am focusing on

- Stable power-stage integration
- Measurement-friendly hardware layout
- Repeatable startup and shutdown procedures
- Easier transfer from simulation assumptions to lab implementation

## Current direction

The next step is to keep improving observability and documentation so this platform becomes a strong baseline for future experiments instead of a single-use prototype.
