## Why this project matters

High-voltage SiC modules can open important operating windows for medium-frequency systems, but only if packaging and stress management are treated as first-class design problems.

## What I am focusing on

- Medium-frequency operation under high-voltage constraints
- Packaging concepts that reduce electric-field concentration
- Integration decisions that support realistic converter architectures
- System-level usefulness rather than isolated component metrics

## Current direction

The current goal is to clarify how SiC module design can support reliable medium-frequency operation and where the biggest integration bottlenecks still remain.
