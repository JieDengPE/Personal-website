## Why this project mattered

This project marked the transition from hands-on hardware work into publication-backed research. It focused on how switching-frequency control and soft-switching techniques could improve high-frequency three-level inverter performance.

## What was completed

- Completed a research line on a three-phase high-frequency T-type three-level inverter
- Developed control strategies around ZVS and variable switching-frequency operation
- Produced journal and conference publications from the work

## Outcome

This completed project became the strongest bridge between earlier converter engineering practice and later medium-voltage research. It also established reusable research methods in writing, validation, and technical framing.
