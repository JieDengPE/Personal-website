## Why this project mattered

Magnetics is a core bottleneck in high-frequency and SST-oriented hardware. This research line focused on transformer structure and magnetic materials as first-class design problems rather than passive supporting details.

## What was completed

- Completed publication-backed work on planar medium-frequency transformer structures
- Studied hybrid magnetic-core concepts for loss reduction and performance improvement
- Extended the work into integrated inductive power and capacitive data transfer structures

## Outcome

This completed project now stands as a distinct magnetic-design branch with published outputs, even though the broader SST research direction continues to evolve.
