## Why this project matters

Power-electronics design often involves many coupled decisions across topology, insulation, switching, packaging, and system targets. AI may help search those spaces faster, but only if the problem is framed with the right engineering structure.

## What I am focusing on

- How AI can support early-stage architecture choices
- Constraint-aware exploration for converters and modules
- Practical use cases that still keep hardware realism in view
- Better ways to combine engineering intuition with automated search

## Current direction

This work is still exploratory, but the goal is clear: turn AI into a useful research tool for faster and better-informed power-electronics design.
