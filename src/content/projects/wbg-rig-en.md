## Why this project mattered

Medium-voltage power semiconductor switching frequency has been difficult to push beyond about 1 MHz. Breaking that barrier can open the door to much higher power density in future medium-voltage electrical equipment.

## What was completed

- Led the project direction
- Built and validated a 1300 V / 60 A GaN power module
- Used a series-stacked GaN device scheme to reach medium-voltage capability

## Outcome

This first-stage project completed the discrete-device module demonstration and produced a conference publication. It now serves as the completed foundation for future bare-die GaN work rather than an active unfinished project.
